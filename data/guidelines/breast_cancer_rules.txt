BREAST CANCER REDCAP ABSTRACTION GUIDELINES
(Synthetic — based on general oncology principles)

RULE 001 — COMORBIDITIES
Include comorbidities ONLY if documented by MD
in first 3-6 months from diagnosis date.
Source must be MD progress note only.
Do not abstract from nursing notes or pharmacy records.

RULE 002 — PRIMARY SITE
Breast cancer primary site must include:
- Laterality (left/right/bilateral)
- Quadrant location (upper outer, upper inner,
  lower outer, lower inner, central/subareolar)
- Clock position if documented
- Distance from nipple if documented

RULE 003 — PATHOLOGY MARKERS
Must abstract all IHC markers:
- ER status (positive/negative + percentage)
- PR status (positive/negative + percentage)
- HER2 status (positive/negative + equivocal)
- Ki-67 percentage if documented
- Molecular subtype if documented

RULE 004 — STAGING
TNM staging must be confirmed by BOTH:
1. Imaging confirmation AND
2. MD documentation
If only one source — mark as CLINICAL staging only.
Record T, N, M separately.
Record overall stage group.

RULE 005 — GERMLINE TESTING
Abstract germline results only from
official genetic testing report.
Do not abstract from MD notes alone.
Record: gene tested, variant found, classification.

RULE 006 — MEDICATIONS / LINE OF TREATMENT
Abstract medications from MD notes ONLY.
Not from pharmacy records.
Not from nursing notes.
Record line of treatment (1st line, 2nd line etc).
Record intent (neoadjuvant, adjuvant, palliative).
Record drug names and regimen.

RULE 007 — IMAGING CONFIRMATION
Breast mass requires BIRADS score for confirmation.
BIRADS 4 or 5 = suspicious/malignant = confirmed.
BIRADS 1-3 = not confirmed malignant on imaging alone.
Record all imaging dates and findings separately.

RULE 008 — DATE FIELDS
Date of diagnosis = date of pathology confirmation.
NOT date of imaging.
NOT date of first symptom.
NOT date of first MD visit.
Date of last contact = last visit OR date of death.

RULE 009 — METASTASIS
Metastasis confirmed only if:
- PET-CT shows FDG avid lesion outside breast/nodes
- AND MD documents as confirmed metastatic disease
Record each metastatic site separately.

RULE 010 — PERFORMANCE STATUS
Abstract ECOG score from MD notes only.
Record at each visit where documented.
Track changes over time.
